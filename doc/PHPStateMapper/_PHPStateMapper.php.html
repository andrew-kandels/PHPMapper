PHPStateMapper

PHPStateMapper is a class for generating a map of the United States with states shaded darker to represent data in a report. It can be thought of as a visual interpretation of "usage by state" and would be an ideal candidate for use in reports on sales, usage, adoption, traffic, and so forth.
